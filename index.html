Who We Are

A female-owned business powering commerce for the builders of tomorrow

MoneyPoint Solutions was founded by a woman who understood firsthand the challenges that small and mid-sized businesses face when navigating payment processing. The hidden fees. The confusing statements. The impersonal service. We set out to change all of that.

We believe that every business — from the neighborhood coffee shop to the growing multi-location retail brand — deserves the same quality of payment tools and the same level of honest, transparent service. No compromise on reliability, no sky-high fees, and no confusing contracts.

Today, MoneyPoint Solutions serves merchants across retail, hospitality, healthcare, and professional services. Our dedicated team of payment specialists is committed to one mission: making your checkout seamless and your business more profitable.
